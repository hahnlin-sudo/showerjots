Your best ideas don't wait for a pen.

They come when your mind wanders — in the shower, half-asleep, mid-stride. And they vanish just as fast. By the time you reach for your phone, the thought is gone. Not the idea itself, but the way you had it. The exact phrasing. The clarity. That version is lost forever.

What we're building

Paper that remembers.

Write naturally on a waterproof e-ink surface. It feels like paper, works under running water, and doesn't glow or buzz for your attention. Step out of the shower and your notes are already on your phone — organized, timestamped, ready.

How it works

Three steps. Zero friction.

01

Jot it down

Pick up the stylus and write. No buttons, no voice commands, no unlocking anything. Just handwriting on a surface that works under running water.

02

Step out

Your notes sync to your phone the moment you're in range. No tapping, no pairing, no thinking about it. It just happens.

03

It's on your phone

Every idea, captured and timestamped. Send it to Apple Notes, Notion, Obsidian — or let it sit until you're ready to act on it.
